The Last Night on Planet Earth


An artistic visual novel by Dejobaan Games. It tells an interesting story of an alien on an earth about to explode. As the UI Programmer, I am playing with those beautiful and interesting pictures.

On Thin Line


Independent game for 41st Ludum Dare jam. A music runner adventure game with smooth control ,fun gameplay and beautiful. Innovative game category. All Arts Have the Same Nature.

PicoCTF 2018


Our team CryptoKnight from CMU, is developing a game for PicoCTF, the largest hacking competition in the world, a pioneer of competition gamification. Participants will read and answer competition question as a part of gameplay.

Super Cashier


Super Cashier is an extremely funny cashier simulator on Oculus. Become a cashier, scan barcodes on various products, welcome wierd customers and compete with another player!

Trick or Treat


To fight against the ban of Halloween, the player (as a witch) flied in the city, threw her magic candies to crying children to encourage them to fight, and finally led her troop of children to beat the boss.

Eat My Carrot


Eat My Carrot is a two-player PC game developed for the 2018 Global Game Jam within 2 days. It is both exciting and relaxing. Players shoot carrots toward rabbits to gain the love of these lovely creatures~!

Roll It Out!


Roll It Out is a mobile game. Controlling a rolling cube and matching colors in the dynamic colorful world, Roll It Out is strenching for fun experience under simple control.

Sugar Rush


Sugar Rush is a third-person running game on Microsoft Kinect. The boy controlled by the player happenly entered an evil castle on Halloween. He needs to evade monsters, collect candies and finally escape.

Home


Home is a short 360° film made by our team Eleven Eyes. It tells about the daily life of a homeless man. Reflecting reality, different people had different attitudes toward him. The world is not full of love —— but it is neither none.

Wish I Could Pee


Wish I Could Pee is a VR game on HTC Vive, where the player plays the role of a godmother and expels monsters to enable a boy to go to the bathroom. Its style is a combination of horror, humor and fantasy story.

Magic Examination


Magic Examination is an AR game where the player plays as a student of a magic school. To pass an examination, he needs to grow several adorable living magic plants in front of the teacher.

About

Hi! I'm Yu Li, or you can call me Lotus. At present I'm a first-year master student at Entertainment Technology Center, Carnegie Mellon University and after graduation I plan to be a gameplay engineer. I love making games! I program, compose and do game designs. I never spare anything I learned from schools or my life in building these fantastic virtual worlds! You can find most of them above in my portfolio.

In my free time, I enjoy music, literature and philosophy. More than just appreciating others' works, I also create by myself. My music pieces and other works will be managed into my portfolio in the future, but now I'm busy on GAMES. I'm working for a game localization group in order to spread game spirits; and I have got several good ideas for my next game! One thing for sure, I will never stop making games;)
