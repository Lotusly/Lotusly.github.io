Roll It Out

  • Platform: Mobile
  • Team size: 1 (Independent Project)
  • Role: Programmer, Designer

Roll It Out is a mobile game with simple machanism. I came out of the idea in 2017 and it is still under development now. The player controls a cube with 6 different colors on its 6 faces, rolling on the colorful ground and avoid dark holes. By matching colors with the color plates on the ground, the player can win both scores and energy for two special skills: "large jump" and "regrowth". They can be useful to help the cube out of danger.

The world is endless; the difficulty is increasing with time. It is not easy to survive the whole piece of music. For now, the basic machanism, progression and skills (everything discribed above) have been completed. I am working on bug fixing, polishing and exploring more interesting features.
