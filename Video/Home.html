Home

  • Team size: 5
  • Role: Composer, Designer
  • Project Session Time: 2 Weeks

* Drag to look around

Home is a short 360° film made by my team. It tells about the daily life of a homeless man. Reflecting reality, different people had different attitudes toward him. The world is not full of love —— but it is neither none.

My major work in this project is composing based on the film structure. As the visual and audio essences follow each other, audience's emotions are aroused most.
